title: Full Outer Join
---

import CodeWithExample from '../../../src/components/code-with-example'

Joins two tables showing all rows, with null values for missing data.
<br/><br/>

<CodeWithExample>

<div/>

```qsl
select * from Users u full outer join Purchases p on u.user_handle = p.userhandle;
```

</CodeWithExample>

Results in data showing only users who have Purchase records.
